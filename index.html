Welcome to Soumya Enterprises - Where Your Vision Meets Excellence. Explore Our Premier Solutions Today!

Soumya Enterprises, established in 2004 by Mr. Mendu Murali, is a professional engineering contracting company specializing in construction with a vision of excellence and environmental harmony. With over 14 years of civil engineering experience, Mr. Murali recognized the need for dedicated and professional project management.

Our mission is to ensure optimal efficiency, cost savings, and environmental protection through modern building practices, renewable energy use, and integral planning. We specialize in luxury residential villas, apartments, commercial, and industrial buildings, and have diversified into paver supply, laying, and road works. At Soumya Enterprises, we prioritize quality, safety, and long-term customer satisfaction.
